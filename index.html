Bio

I am Tuan-Anh Vu, a Ph.D. student from the Computer Science and Engineering Department at Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, where I am advised by Assoc. Prof. Sai-Kit Yeung.


Previously, I am a member of Computer Vision and Image Processing Lab under the supervision of Dr. Ha Viet Uyen Synh and also a Research Assistance in the School of Computer Science and Engineering, International University - Vietnam National University Ho Chi Minh City.


My main research interest lies in Deep Learning, 3D Reconstruction, Scene Understanding.
