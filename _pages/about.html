Tuan-Anh Vu

I am a third year CS Ph.D. candidate at Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) advised by Prof. Sai-Kit Yeung. Previously, I am a member of Computer Vision and Image Processing Lab under the supervision of Dr. Ha Viet Uyen Synh and also Research Assistant in the School of Computer Science and Engineering, International University - Vietnam National University Ho Chi Minh City.

I am generally interested in Computer Vision, Graphics and Deep Learning. My recent research focuses on Deep Learning, 3D Reconstruction, Scene Understanding, Shape Analysis, and Geometry Processing.

Currently, I have the pleasure of working with Dr. Duc Thanh Nguyen, Dr. Binh-Son Hua, and Dr. Quang-Hieu Pham

Check my CV for more details.
