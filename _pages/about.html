Tuan-Anh Vu

I'm currently a Postdoctoral Researcher at UCLA SCI Lab led by Prof. M. Khalid Jawed. I obtained my PhD in Computer Science and Engineering at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) in 2024, advised by Prof. Sai-Kit Yeung. Previously, I was a member of Computer Vision and Image Processing Lab under the supervision of Dr. Ha Viet Uyen Synh and also Research Assistant in the School of Computer Science and Engineering, International University - Vietnam National University Ho Chi Minh City.

I am very grateful to be a recipient of the A*STAR Research Attachment Programme (ARAP), which supports my research for 2 years at Centre for Frontier AI Research, A*STAR. During this time, I am very fortunate to work and be mentored by Prof. Qing Guo and Prof. Ivor W. Tsang.

My recent research focuses on Robot Perception and Navigation, Deep Learning, 3D Reconstruction, Scene Understanding, Shape Analysis, and Geometry Processing. Currently, I have a pleasure of working with Prof. Duc Thanh Nguyen, Prof. Binh-Son Hua, Dr. Quang-Hieu Pham, and Prof. Zhiyuan Zhang.

Check my lastest CV, Google Scholar and PhD dissertation for more details.
