Tuan-Anh Vu

I am a CS Ph.D. candidate at Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) advised by Prof. Sai-Kit Yeung. Previously, I am a member of Computer Vision and Image Processing Lab under the supervision of Dr. Ha Viet Uyen Synh and also Research Assistant in the School of Computer Science and Engineering, International University - Vietnam National University Ho Chi Minh City.

I am very grateful to be a recipient of the A*STAR Research Attachment Programme (ARAP), which supports my research for 18 months at Centre for Frontier AI Research, A*STAR. During this time, I am very fortunate to work and be mentored by Dr. Qing Guo and Prof. Ivor W Tsang.

My recent research focuses on Deep Learning, 3D Reconstruction, Scene Understanding, Shape Analysis, and Geometry Processing. Currently, I have a pleasure of working with Prof. Duc Thanh Nguyen, Prof. Binh-Son Hua, and Dr. Quang-Hieu Pham.
